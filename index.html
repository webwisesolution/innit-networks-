Our Story

Founded in 2020, INNIT NETWORKS began as a small podcast among friends passionate about technology, culture, and the African digital renaissance. What started as casual conversations has grown into a vibrant community of over 12,000 members across the continent.

We believe in the power of African voices shaping the future of technology. Our mission is to create a platform where ideas are shared, connections are made, and innovation is nurtured.

Our Mission

To democratize tech education and foster a community-driven approach to digital innovation in Africa. We're building bridges between emerging talents and established industry leaders.

Our Vision

A future where African tech creators are at the forefront of global innovation, supported by a strong, interconnected community that shares knowledge and opportunities.
